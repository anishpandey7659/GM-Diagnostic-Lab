FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions we receive from our patients.

Most routine test reports are delivered the same day via email and WhatsApp. Advanced tests may take 24–72 hours depending on the test type. You can also download reports from your patient portal at any time.
You can book instantly through our website's Book Appointment form, via WhatsApp, or by calling our helpline. We also welcome walk-ins, though appointments help reduce your wait time significantly.
Yes, we provide free home sample collection within the city. Our trained phlebotomists carry sanitized kits and follow strict safety protocols. Home collection is available from 6:30 AM to 7:00 PM daily.
We accept cash, UPI, all major credit/debit cards, net banking, and popular mobile wallets. EMI options are available for premium health packages and corporate clients.
Preparation varies by test. Fasting blood tests require 8–12 hours of fasting with only water allowed. We send a detailed preparation guide via SMS and email as soon as you book your appointment.
Yes, we are empaneled with most major insurance providers and TPAs. Please share your insurance details during booking for cashless processing where applicable.
